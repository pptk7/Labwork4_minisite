Curriculum Overview (5 Years)

Year 1: Fundamentals

Focus on Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Basic Engineering skills.





Year 2: Advanced Sciences

Advanced Calculus, Mechanics, and Introduction to specialized fields.





Year 3: Specialization

Students choose their specific engineering track and start project-based learning.

Note: Students must pass all core subjects to proceed.





Year 4: Research & Development

Intensive laboratory work and internship preparation.





Year 5: Innovation & Internship

Final project and industrial internship.
